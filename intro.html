About Our Team

We are the ICT Club from Oromia Development Association Special Boarding School in Bishoftu, Ethiopia. Our team consists of passionate students from grades Bishoftu ODA SBS who share a common interest in technology and innovation.

Under the guidance of our ICT teacher, Mr. Misgana Ayana, we've developed the Smart Student Package to help students excel in their studies through digital tools and resources. Our mission is to leverage technology to solve educational challenges and make learning more accessible and engaging for all students.

This project represents months of hard work, collaboration, and dedication to creating tools that we ourselves would find useful in our daily studies.

About the Smart Student Package

The Smart Student Package is a comprehensive collection of digital tools designed to support students in various aspects of their academic journey. From study aids to productivity tools, each feature has been carefully crafted to address specific educational needs.

Smart Reading

A reading companion that lets you choose subjects, plays focus-enhancing audio, and includes a countdown timer to support productive study sessions.

Weekly Study Planner

A personal planner that lets you add, edit, and delete daily tasks. Track completion with visual indicators. Save progress via localStorage with reminders and priority settings.

Your AI Advisor

Acts as a motivational and educational guide—sharing success stories, offering academic advice, and encouraging students to strive for excellence.

Smart Notar

Automatically generates lesson notes, summaries, and explanations using AI to help you quickly grasp key concepts from your study materials.

Smart AI Notar

An AI-powered assistant that simplifies study materials, highlights key points, and organizes content for easier understanding and retention.

Smart Deep Study

A focused study assistant designed to enhance concentration through structured time blocks, motivation boosters, and reflection prompts.

Learn Web Development

Beginner-friendly resource to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with code examples, layout guides, and mobile-friendly design.

Learn Python

Interactive platform to learn Python programming from basics to advanced topics like variables, syntax, control flow, and data types.

Spelling Bee Challenge

Interactive vocabulary game with hints, definitions, synonyms, and score tracking. Includes easy/medium/hard levels and subject categories.
